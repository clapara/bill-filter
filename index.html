Database: Lawsuits against anti-trans bills

by Cailley LaPara

Over the past two years, numerous states have introduced or enacted bills targeting trans people, restricting their access to things from healthcare to school sports. In response, civil rights organizations are fighting anti-trans legislation in court.

This database attempts to keep track of those legal battles. While it is not yet complete, users can search for lawsuits by state and status.

The hope is that this database would help advocates keep up with the uphill battle for trans rights being fought across the country.
